About Us

Established in 2011, NANOTEC has positioned itself as a cutting-edge technology company primarily dedicated to the commercialization of a wide range of IoT products.

It specializes in the implementation and development of luxury smart spaces, encompassing residential projects such as buildings, apartments, and houses, as well as commercial environments including offices and commercial premises.

Its focus seeks to transform these spaces through the integration of advanced technological solutions, elevating their functionality, comfort, and sophistication.
